PLC

PLC Overview & Hardware Components

PLC Overview & Hardware Components, Number Systems and Codes, Fundamentals of Logic, Basics of PLC Programming Developing Fundamental PLC Wiring Diagrams and Ladder Logic Programs, Programming Timers, Programming Counters, Program Control Instructions, Data Manipulation Instructions, Math Instructions, Sequencer and Shift Register Instructions, PLC Installation Practices, Editing, and Troubleshooting

HMI

HMI Display Design

HMI Display Design, HMI Display Color Guidelines, HMI Display Navigation HMI File and Object Naming Conventions, Alarm & Event Views, HMI Display Scripting, Introduction FactoryTalk, Ignition & WinCC HMI Display Building Platforms

EtherNet/IP Modbus, OPC, DeviceNet, ControlNet, Fieldbus – Intro, PROFIBUS-DP - Intro

SCADA

Remote Telemetry Units (RTUs), Human Machine Interface, Communications Network, Introduction to Ignition SCADA, Introduction to Open Source SCADA

Introduction to DCS

This module is intended to familiarize the student with the most important aspects of Distributed Control Systems. Topics covered in the module include DCS Controllers, DCS I/Os, HMIs & DCS Control Network.

We Adopt a school type approach where theories will be blended with practicals. Our LMS (Learning Management System) is deployed in the cloud to aid this approach. Also we provide a Library Portal loaded with tech related books and study materials for student research and private studies.

Well Guided Program

Live Virtual Class

This Phase Consist of Online live Virtual Classes, modularised lessons managed by Learning Management System with assignments, downloadable study materials and video lectures to fully engage the student on weekly basis.

Lab work after each key concept is taught.

Instructor Guided Practicals.

Performance and assessment grading based on assignments and lab work.

Physical Lab Workshop

Physical Lab Workshop

On completion of the three months of live virtual classes and lab work, students are to visit any of our physical labs for a two-day physical workshop.

Three Months Project Based Learning

The student will gain knowledge and skills by working for 3 months to investigate and respond with a solution to an authentic industry-related problem. The students will engage with project management tools to enable them to gain an understanding of tasks, timelines, schedules with a project manager assigned.

The learning will mirror what professionals do every day and prepares the students to accept and meet challenges in the real world.

We offer blended training where theories are thought in classes via our Virtual Meeting Platform and practical's are done physically in our training centers. We offer our graduates project based experience in the course of their training at Madesoft Academy as such we can guarantee their work experience to potential employer.

Admission Requirments

Applicant for this course must have a minimum of Diploma in any of the sciences or currently in an institution of Higher learning. Also Applicant without qualification but have 1+ year working experience in the field of electrical/electronics or computer shall be considered for a place in the program.

To participate in this program students must have a personal computer that meets the following minimum specifications: Intel Pentium or equivalent for Virtual Machine & Simulator

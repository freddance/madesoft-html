How It works

This Industrial Automation and PLC Course is divided into 2 Phases

3 Months Intensive Lessons

This Program Consist of Online Virtual Classes, modularised lessons managed by Learning Management System with assignments, downloadable study materials and video lectures to fully engage the student on weekly basis.

Student will be involved in lab work after each key concept is taught, the lab work shall be in the form of practicals with guide from instructors, each assignment and lab work shall be used as part of performance and assessment grading.

3 Months Project Based Learning

Student will gain knowledge and skills by working for 3 months to investigate and respond with a solution to an authentic industry related problem. Student shall engage with project management tools to enable them gain understanding of tasks, timelines, schedules with a project Manager assigned.

The learning will mirror what professionals do every day and prepares the students to accept and meet challenges in the real world
